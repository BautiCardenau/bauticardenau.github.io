Let's talk a
bit about me

My two passions are Technology and Business. This is the main reason why I enrolled in Industrial Engineering at Instituto Tecnológico de Buenos Aires. However, a while back I noticed that my degree did not cover a topic I was keen on learning: Programming. Thus, I decided to change my situation and started learning Android Development. Smartphones are crucial to us nowadays, and they have enormous potential.

While learning about this new domain, I also started with Python. I had the chance to put these skills into practice during a course I took on my exchange program at Eindhoven Institute of Technology. The course was called Advanced Simulation and it consisted of writing computer simulations for a variety of real-life processes that exhibit stochastic behavior, ranging from services, highway networks, to epidemic models.

I believe a problem-solving mindset is vital. We are in constant contact with conflicts, even though we wished for the opposite. Therefore, one needs to find a solution to them without feeling anxious, stressed out, or demoralized. As the Stoics would say: the obstacle is the way.

“The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way.” - Marcus Aurelius
